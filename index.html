Welcome

We are a community of dota players from all MMRs looking to have a good time. If your looking for a teamspeak server to use while playing dota this is the place for you. We have multiple ranks for how long you have been with us from gg branch up to rapier for server admins.

About

We are a primarily english speaking teamspeak server but do have a good number of german speakers also. You can join by typing in ts.pudge.pro to your teamspeak client.

Competitive Team and Improvement

We have an active team that play regulrarly in competitive tournaments for fun. If you would like to meet up with other like minded people and form a team this could be the right place for that. Try hanging around for abit and joining some games. Messing around isn't for everyone and improving at the games does give a lot of satisfaction.

Bring your friends

We have plenty of space so bring your friends and lets make this a great dota centred teamspeak for everybody.
